## Naming Guidelines for Derivative Works

If you create a modified version or fork of Fincept Terminal, you must:

### 1. **Choose a Distinct Name**
   Your project name should:
   - Be clearly different from "Fincept Terminal" or "Fincept"
   - Not suggest official endorsement
   - Not create confusion in the marketplace

   **Good Examples:**
   - ✅ "QuantTerminal" (based on Fincept Terminal)
   - ✅ "TradeDesk Pro" (fork of Fincept Terminal)
   - ✅ "AlphaAnalyzer" (powered by Fincept Terminal)

   **Bad Examples:**
   - ❌ "Fincept Community Edition"
   - ❌ "Fincept Terminal Plus"
   - ❌ "Fincept Terminal Lite"

### 2. **Provide Clear Attribution**
   Include a notice in your documentation:
   ```
   This software is based on Fincept Terminal
   (https://github.com/Fincept-Corporation/FinceptTerminal)
   developed by Fincept Corporation.

   [Your Project Name] is not affiliated with, endorsed by,
   or sponsored by Fincept Corporation.
   ```

### 3. **Use Distinctive Visual Identity**
   - Create your own logo and color scheme
   - Do not copy or closely imitate Fincept's visual design
   - Ensure users can easily distinguish your product

## Logo Usage Guidelines

If you have permission to use the Fincept logo (e.g., as a partner or contributor):

### Specifications

1. **Minimum Size**
   - Print: 0.5 inches (12.7mm) width minimum
   - Digital: 120px width minimum

2. **Clear Space**
   - Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "F" in Fincept
   - No text, graphics, or other elements in this zone

3. **Color Variants**
   - Primary: Full color on white background
   - Secondary: White logo on dark background
   - Tertiary: Black logo on light background

4. **Prohibited Modifications**
   - ❌ Do not rotate, skew, or distort
   - ❌ Do not change colors or add effects
   - ❌ Do not recreate or redraw
   - ❌ Do not use outdated versions
